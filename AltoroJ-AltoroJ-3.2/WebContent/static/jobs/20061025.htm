Loyalty Marketing Program Manager

Category:Marketing
Date:Oct-25-2006

This position is responsible for all direct mail campaigns as part of our Credit Card Loyalty Marketing program. The Loyalty Marketing Program Manager is expected to:

  • Develop innovative creative concepts
  • Manage all aspects of the production process
  • Manage all aspects of the compliance process including submitting creative pieces for legal approval and maintaining documentation surrounding all programs and campaigns

Strong relationship management skills are essential for the success of this position. External contacts may include senior managers at current and prospective affinity partners, print vendors, and enhancement vendors.

Required Skills:

  • Previous marketing or marketing production experience
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong problem solving or analytical skills

Teller

Category:Banking
Date:Oct-19-2006

Note: this is a full-time position, offering 40 hours per week, Monday-Saturday.

Build customer loyalty through courtesy and friendliness while providing basic teller services. The Teller is expected to:

  • Ensure accurate and timely processing of transactions
  • Resolve basic service issues to guarantee customer satisfaction
  • Via customer education and cross-sell referrals, promote consumer services and products
  • Follow all bank fraud prevention and security procedures
  • Assist other associates as needed

Required Skills:

  • At least one year of customer service or sales experience
  • Excellent inter-personal skills

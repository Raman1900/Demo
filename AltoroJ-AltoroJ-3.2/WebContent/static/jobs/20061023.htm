Executive Assistant

Category:Administrative
Date:Oct-23-2006

The main purpose of this position is to relieve management of administrative details. You will be expected to answer calls; coordinate schedules, meetings, travel; schedule conference calls and meeting rooms; order supplies, etc.).

You will perform advanced administrative support functions including composing, signing and releasing correspondence. Possessing the ability to maintain a high level of confidentiality is required. Further, you may be involved in high-level client contact and exposure to sensitive information, and must use considerable tact, diplomacy and judgment.

Finally, you will be required to juggle a number of different and often conflicting activities at one time. Time management skills will be essential.

Required Skills:

  • Proficiency in Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Strong communication skills
  • Strong team player

Management Trainee Program

Take Advantage of Altoro Mutual’s Management Trainee Program

Would you like help finding the perfect career? Then, apply for Altoro Mutual's 18-month Management Trainee Program. While networking with senior executives, you will have the opportunity to experience how each of our businesses work. Here is just a sampling of the experiences you might gain:

  • Rotate through key business units
  • Work on special projects designed to give you valuable work experience
  • Meet & interview senior executives
  • Network with a large group of trainee alumnae
  • Receive career guidance through individual development planning
  • Attend seminars about the banking industry

Candidate Requirements

Altoro Mutual’s Management Trainee program recruits top individuals who meet the following requirements:

  • Degree from accredited 4-year institution of higher learning (Any field of study)
  • Cumulative GPA of 3.5 or above
  • Excellent written & oral communication skills
  • Demonstrated leadership in campus & community organizations
  • A positive attitude & desire for a bright future

What happens after your complete the Management Trainee Program? You determine that! The opportunities at Altoro Mutual are as diverse as its employees.

To apply, select "Management Trainee Program" from our current job openings list.

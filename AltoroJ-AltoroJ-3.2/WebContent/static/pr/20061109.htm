Altoro Mutual Announces Earnings Webcast & Teleconference Call for Third Quarter 2006 Financial Results

Oct-5-2006

Altoro Mutual will hold a presentation for the investment community on Thursday, October 26, 2006, at 11:00 a.m. ET (8:00 a.m. PT) to review third quarter 2006 financial results, which are scheduled to be released before the market opens that morning. A live webcast of the presentation will be hosted by Karl Fitzgerald, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer. A slide presentation will accompany the webcast and will be available on our home page at the time of the call.
